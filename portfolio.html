Blooming Therapy Co.

Type: Logo design
Vibes: Rustic, warm, inviting, feminine

Blooming Therapy Co. is a small business based out of California that specializes in various forms of in-home therapy. The client wanted something rustic and warm while also being slighlty feminine as their target audience is mostly women. Inspriation was drawn from the mountains and deserts in the region and a rose design was added to play off the "blooming" aspect of their business.

Small World of Destrehan

Type: Logo design
Vibes: Childish, friendly, playful, fun

Small World of Destrehan is a small buisness in Louisiana that specializes in early childhood education (i.e. daycare). The client wanted something friendly and playful that was a representation of their business but also their clientel (babies and toddlers). You'll notice the "small world" in between the children that speaks to the brand name.

Touch of Healthy

Type: Logo design
Vibes: Bright, green, clean, relateable

Touch of Healthy was a health and wellness blog I created as part of a person project. Things shifted in my life and I eventually moved away from the blog, but I wanted to create something that gave those health and wellness vibes. You can also view the website template I created here.
